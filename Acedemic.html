Overview

We at Stan Group feel extremely privileged to have been able to provide manpower resources to drive the country's growth engine. since the year 1996, the group has placed more than 80% students in various companies.

Stan University has a sprawling campus spread across 63 acres land. Its state-of-art facilities give opportunities to develop leadership skills and to achieve professional excellence. It has 12,000+ students from 85+ countries, 29 states, and Union Territories, providing cultural diversity and global exposure to students. University has top-of-the-line labs and live Projects IBM labs, the center of competency in VLSI Design to name a few.

One of the biggest boosts from college is an increase in self-confidence. The interesting fact is when you join the college in first year you are amateur but by the end of final year, your personality changes drastically. You gain high self-esteem, more knowledge, develop good networking and industry exposure. As you study at Stan University gradually confidence develops naturally.
